WEB SYSTEMS



Automated Fare and Passenger Management System

The created fare and passenger management system is a web-based system that has registration for three (3) different users: Admin, Driver, and Passenger. It has all the capabilities, such as automating the traditional way of paying fares in commuting using QR codes and built-in QR Scanner, reducing the delay of their time, can calculate exact amount of fare, students/senior citizen/PWD discount, and saved a lot of time either collecting fare or processing reports. The system can also be used to track number of customers and transactions in a daily basis. The level of the usability of the Automated Fare Management in terms of design, functionality, usability and security was very acceptable.
